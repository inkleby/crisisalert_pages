It is ironic that today we have seen headlines describing the last echo - perhaps not the last - of the Fringe Banking Crisis and crash of 1972–73.

Debate On The Address

Mr. Frank Hooley - May 15, 1979

Over the past couple of years we have had the Fringe Banking Crisis, the sterling crisis and the scandal of the Crown Agents.

Select Committees

Mr. Frank Hooley - June 25, 1979

It is generally recognised that some of the inadequacies now apparent in the Banking Act 1979 are due to the fact that it was designed specifically to redress the problems of the Fringe Banking Crisis of the early 1970s.
